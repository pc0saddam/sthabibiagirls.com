About Us

St. Habibia Girls Educating Girls, Building Strong Futures

St. Habibia Girls is a dedicated institution committed to providing quality education for girls in a disciplined, safe, and inspiring environment. We believe every girl deserves equal opportunity to learn, grow, and succeed.

Our focus is not only on academic excellence but also on character building, moral values, confidence, and leadership. We encourage students to become responsible citizens with strong knowledge, skills, and Islamic values.
